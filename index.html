About us

Snapsweats is a company dedicated to transforming fashion in a sustainable and personalized way. Our hoodies are not only a statement of style, but also a commitment to the environment. We use high quality recycled materials to create comfortable and modern garments. What makes us unique is our customization platform, giving our customers the freedom to wear the hoodies to their liking. In addition, we are committed to reducing textile waste. We accept and recycle second-hand clothes, giving new life to forgotten garments. Thus, we not only create fashion, but also contribute to a more circular and sustainable fashion cycle.
